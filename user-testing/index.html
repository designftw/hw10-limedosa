General Takeaways:

  • The simplification of the app worked to the favor of me. This included:
    • simplification of the general idea => making this an app to text friends and family rather than an app to text even co-workers. Unfortunately the initial idea wasn't as fruitful as initially believed.
    • simplification of the design => making the design more minimalistic and less cluttered. This was done by simplifying and erasing other features of the initial prototypes.
  • Users have an easier time grasping the idea of grouping within the app. The filtering makes more sense to them.
  • The design is simple and related to the real world enough to be easy to navigate; it will be kept.
  • In the future, expanding on pages such as the adding a post or sharing a post page would be fruitful for the eventual design of the app.
